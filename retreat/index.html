Stunning Southern Spain

The retreat is in the stunning Suryalila retreat centre (where we both trained and Joanna is now living!) - right in the heart of the Andalusian countryside ‚ô• an area known for its natural beauty. The majestic mountain range of Sierra de Grazalema can be seen on the horizon behind Suryalila, making the views in all directions breathtaking.
