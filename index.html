Stunning Southern Spain

The retreat is in the stunning Suryalila retreat centre (where we both trained and Joanna is now living!) - right in the heart of the Andalusian countryside ‚ô• an area known for its natural beauty. The majestic mountain range of Sierra de Grazalema can be seen on the horizon behind Suryalila, making the views in all directions breathtaking.

The property has 50 acres of land and is still a working olive farm with 800 olive trees. The buildings are beautifully renovated farm houses laid out in typical Spanish style with lemon and orange tree courtyards.

Suryalila also has state of the art yoga halls, including the magnificent Om Dome, with a high quality sound system, beautiful views and decorated murals around the walls. We can't wait to host our ecstatic dance in this amazing space!!
